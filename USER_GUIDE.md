## Getting Started

### Accessing the Platform

Visit [aaocab.com](https://aaocab.com) on any device - desktop, tablet, or mobile. The website is fully responsive and works on all modern browsers.

### Do I Need an Account?

- **Without an account**: You can make bookings and receive confirmation via WhatsApp
- **With an account**: Track all bookings, make online payments, manage your profile, and access booking history

## Creating an Account

### Registration Steps

1. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Register"** in the navigation bar
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Check your email for a verification link
5. Click the verification link to activate your account

### Email Verification

- A verification email will be sent to your registered email
- Click the link in the email within 24 hours
- If you don't receive it, check your spam folder
- Use the **"Resend Verification"** option if needed

### Password Requirements

- Minimum 6 characters
- Mix of letters and numbers recommended
- Avoid common passwords

## Booking a Cab

### Step-by-Step Booking Process

1. **Select Trip Type**
   - **One Way**: Single journey from A to B
   - **Round Trip**: Go and return journey
   - **Rental**: Hourly/daily rental with driver

2. **Enter Locations**
   - Type your pickup location - suggestions will appear
   - Type your drop location
   - Select from the autocomplete suggestions

3. **Choose Date and Time**
   - Select your trip date (advance booking required)
   - Choose pickup time

4. **Select Vehicle**
   - **Sedan**: Up to 4 passengers, basic comfort
   - **Sedan XL**: Up to 4 passengers, extra legroom
   - **SUV**: Up to 6 passengers, more luggage space
   - **Innova**: Up to 7 passengers, family trips
   - **Tempo Traveller**: Up to 12 passengers, group travel
   - **Mini Bus**: Up to 20 passengers, large groups

5. **Add Special Requirements** (optional)
   - Baby on board - child seat arrangement
   - Patient on board - extra care during journey
   - Pet on board - pet-friendly vehicle

6. **Enter Contact Details**
   - Your name
   - Phone number (required for driver coordination)
   - Email (optional, for booking confirmation)

7. **Review and Confirm**
   - Check the estimated fare
   - Review all details
   - Click **"Book Now"**

### After Booking

- You'll see a confirmation with your booking ID
- If logged in, the booking appears in "My Bookings"
- You can pay online or choose to pay later
- Our team will confirm the booking via call/WhatsApp

## Managing Your Bookings

### Viewing Your Bookings

1. Log in to your account
2. Click **"My Bookings"** in the navigation
3. See all your bookings with status:
   - **Pending** - Awaiting confirmation
   - **Confirmed** - Booking confirmed
   - **Cancelled** - Booking cancelled

### Booking Details

Click on any booking to see:
- Trip route and locations
- Date and time
- Vehicle type
- Fare breakdown
- Payment status
- Contact information

### Cancelling a Booking

1. Go to "My Bookings"
2. Find the booking you want to cancel
3. Click **"Cancel"** button
4. Confirm cancellation

**Cancellation Policy:**
- Free cancellation up to 4 hours before trip
- Confirmed bookings can be cancelled up to 4 hours prior
- Refunds for paid bookings processed within 5-7 days

## Making Payments

### Payment Options

1. **Pay Online** - Secure payment via Stripe
   - Credit/Debit Cards
   - UPI (in supported regions)
   - Net Banking

2. **Pay Later** - Cash to driver after trip

### Online Payment Steps

1. Go to your booking details
2. Click **"Pay Now"** button
3. Enter your card details
4. Click **"Pay"**
5. Wait for confirmation
6. Download receipt if needed

### Payment Status

- **Pending** - Payment not yet made
- **Paid** - Payment successful
- **Refunded** - Amount refunded

### Downloading Receipt

After successful payment:
1. Go to payment success page or booking details
2. Click **"Download Receipt"**
3. Save the receipt for your records

## Your Profile

### Accessing Profile

1. Click on your name or **"Profile"** in navigation
2. Access two tabs: **Profile** and **Security**

### Updating Profile Information

1. Go to Profile tab
2. Update your details:
   - Full Name
   - Phone Number
3. Click **"Update Profile"**

### Changing Password

1. Go to Security tab
2. Enter your new password
3. Confirm the new password
4. Click **"Update Password"**

### Password Recovery

If you forgot your password:
1. Click **"Forgot Password"** on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **"Send Reset Link"**
4. Check your email for reset link
5. Click the link and set new password

## FAQs

### Booking Questions

**Q: How far in advance should I book?**
A: We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for guaranteed availability. For peak times and holidays, book 2-3 days ahead.

**Q: Can I modify my booking after confirmation?**
A: Yes, contact our support team via WhatsApp or phone to make changes. Changes depend on availability.

**Q: What if I need to book immediately?**
A: For urgent bookings, call us directly at +91 78903 02302. We'll try our best to accommodate.

### Payment Questions

**Q: Is online payment safe?**
A: Yes, we use Stripe for secure payment processing. Your card details are encrypted and never stored on our servers.

**Q: When will I get my refund?**
A: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.

**Q: Can I pay cash?**
A: Yes, you can choose to pay cash to the driver after your trip.

### Trip Questions

**Q: Will the driver contact me?**
A: Yes, the assigned driver will call you 30 minutes before pickup to confirm.

**Q: What if my flight is delayed?**
A: For airport pickups, inform us immediately. We monitor flights and adjust accordingly.

**Q: Can I request a specific driver?**
A: While we can't guarantee specific drivers, you can mention preferences in special requirements.

### Account Questions

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact our support team to request account deletion. Note that this will remove all your booking history.

**Q: I didn't receive the verification email?**
A: Check spam folder. If not there, use "Resend Verification" option or contact support.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: We recommend one account per person. Multiple accounts may cause confusion in booking management.
